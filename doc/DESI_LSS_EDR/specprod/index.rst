========
specprod
========

:envvar:`specprod` defines the spectroscopic production run. For EDR, we use ``fuji``, named after the mountain and signifying the sixth internal release of data. More information can be found in `DESI Collaboration, in preparation (2023)`_.  

Here ``release`` defines the LSS-defined selection of tiles. For the EDR, it represents the One-Percent Survey named as :envvar:`sv3`.

.. _DESI Collaboration, in preparation (2023): https://ref_EDR
