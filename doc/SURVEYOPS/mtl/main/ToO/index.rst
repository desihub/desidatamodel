===
ToO
===

The ``ToO`` directory hosts a monolithic ledger of Targets of Opportunity.
This is used by the DESI fiberassign pipeline to assign
special, time-critical, observations.

   :maxdepth: 1

   ToO-input.rst : Minimal information used to derive the Targets of Opportunity ledger.
   ToO.rst : Targets of Opportunity ledger in a form that can be read by fiberassign.
